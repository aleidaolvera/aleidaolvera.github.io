What is TSA?

The Technology Student Association is an organization that promotes leadership and educational growth through engineering, technology, and innovation. The association reinforces these ideologies through competitions and conferences. TSA is made up of 190,000 students across the nation. TSA in integrated with the study of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) and takes its principles outside of the classroom. TSA is a diverse organization, which makes every individual experience a positive and personalized influence on their lives. Visit our chapter's page for more information on TSA.

TSA Chapter News

The Rio Grande Valley Regional VEX Tournament was held at Veterans Memorial High School on February 8, 2014. Two teams from Veterans Memorial competed in the competition, and both are going to be advancing to the state competition held in Waco in April! The Veterans Memorial Team 2 took 1st Place for the Tournament, 1st for Programming, and 1st for Robot Skills. Go Charger TSA!
