WHAT IS FOX DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT?

Fox Design and Development is the professional business of Aleida Olvera. Aleida is a freelance graphic design artist and web developer, as well as an aspiring computer scientist. Aleida created this business in order to generate revenue for her future educational expenses.


WHAT DOES FOX DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT PROVIDE?

Fox Design and Development is eager to take on new projects and will provide the most enjoyable and professional experience while you expand and/or begin your business. From creating logos, to getting your website started, Fox Design and Development will meet all your business' digital needs. Customer service is FD&D's number one priority, so you will be receiving the most attention and flexibility with your project.

Charger History Club

This past year I worked with Veterans Memorial to help begin a club, by creating its identity. I was given the task to create a logo that represented the school and the club's main focus. With this in mind I created the logo below. The logo encompasses the "History Channel's" H within two C's that helps convey the History Club's main purpose: History! The logo was created using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.




This T-Shirt was designed for the Charger History Club's National History Day qualifying team. Along with this, the above shirt was taken to Washington D.C. to identify the students participating.
