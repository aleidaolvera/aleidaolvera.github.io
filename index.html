Welcome!

Hello, I am Aleida Olvera - a young, 17-year-old woman aspiring to become a software engineer and professional graphic desinger. I am currently interning for Patron and Cannon as a freelance UI/UX Designer. I have experience working with Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and Dreamweaver - which I have used to create various design and web development projects. Because of my experience and work ethic I have been awarded and honored to be a part of multiple programs. Growing up in a poor community has allowed me to search for opportunities to shine beyond limitations. Therefore, in my spare time I work to expose others to the opportunities and benefits that come within the field of computing through the upcoming program: SparkIT.

As a young Latina myself, I am eager to embark on any adventures that help advance and inspire other Latinos in my community to pursue fields in STEM. If you are interested in sponsoring any of my upcoming events, programs (such as SparkIT) or inquire about services - do not hesitate to contact me!
