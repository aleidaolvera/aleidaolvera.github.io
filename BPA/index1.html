Welcome!

Hello, I am Aleida Olvera - a young, Hispanic woman aspiring to become a software engineer and professional graphic designer. To take my first steps into pursuing a successful career in the Human-Computer Interaction field In the past I have interned for Patron and Cannon as a UI/UX Designer. Along with this, I am a part of the Executive Team for Everybody Code Now! - a national non-profit that works to empower the next generation of leaders through computing. I have experience working with applications such as AutoCAD, along with Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and Dreamweaver - which I have used to create various design and web development projects. Because of my experience and work ethic I have been awarded and honored to be a part of multiple programs. Growing up in a poor community has allowed me to search for opportunities to shine beyond limitations. Therefore, in my spare time I work to expose others to the opportunities and benefits that come within the field of computing through Girl Who Code, the first chapter in the Rio Grande Valley.
